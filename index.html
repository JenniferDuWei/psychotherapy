Welcome

I am a counsellor offering a warm, reflective, and collaborative space for people who want to understand themselves more deeply and find new ways of being with their experiences.

How I Work

My approach is grounded in person-centred and relational therapy. I value empathy, honesty, and moving at a pace that feels right for you. Therapy is not about fixing, but about making sense of what matters.

Who I Work With

I work with adults experiencing anxiety, low mood, shame, relationship difficulties, and questions around identity and meaning. You do not need to be in crisis to seek therapy.
